What is FIFA 16?

FIFA 16 is a video game based on the worlds most popular sport, Football, or commonly known as soccer in North America. In FIFA 16 the player can play as there favorite soccer team in a variety of game types or even create there own team. Play 1 on 1 online matches against other players in head to head matches, or compete in online divisions with your Ultimate Team. You have friends who own the game as well? Well then you guys can create your own football club and compee against others in Pro Clubs.
